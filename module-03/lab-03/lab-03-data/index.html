Additional information about the data

The state of Kentucky is one of four U.S. states constituted as a commonwealth. The region was originally part of the Commonwealth of Virginia, but became the 15th U.S. state in 1792. Other interesting facts about the Commonwealth of Kentucky include: The state is known to have the worlds longest cave system (Mammoth Cave National Park) A very unique geographical feature is the "Kentucky Bend" which is a portion of the state surrounded by Tennessee and Missouri in the western corner of the state. Access to this exclave of Kentucky is via a road through a portion of Tennessee.
